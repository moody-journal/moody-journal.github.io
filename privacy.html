Privacy Policy

Moody for iOS
Version 1.0
🔒

The short version

Moody collects nothing. All AI analysis runs locally on your iPhone using Apple Intelligence. Your journal entries are never sent to any server, cloud service, or third party. There are no accounts, no tracking, no ads, and no data sales.

01

Overview

Moody is a journaling and mood-tracking application developed for iOS. This Privacy Policy describes how the App handles your information.

The short version: we collect nothing, because the App never contacts our servers with your data.

02

Data we do not collect

The App does not collect, transmit, store, or share:

  • Your journal entries or written content
  • Your mood ratings or emotional data
  • Your name, email address, or any personally identifying information
  • Your location data
  • Your usage patterns, analytics, or crash reports
  • Any data derived from AI analysis of your content
03

On-device processing

All AI-powered features in Moody, including achievement detection and mood analysis, are processed entirely on your device using Apple's on-device Apple Intelligence framework.

Your journal content is never sent to any external server, API, or third-party service for processing.

04

Local storage

Your data, journal entries, mood logs, and awards are stored locally on your device using iOS standard data storage. The App does not have a backend server or database.

If you delete the App, all locally stored data is permanently deleted.

05

iCloud

If you have iCloud Backup enabled on your device, iOS may include Moody's local data in your personal iCloud backup. This is governed by Apple's iCloud Terms of Service and your own iOS settings.

Moody does not independently upload data to iCloud or any other cloud service.

06

Third-party services

The App does not integrate any third-party analytics, advertising, crash reporting, or tracking SDKs. There are no third parties with access to your data through use of this App.

07

Children's privacy

The App does not knowingly collect information from children under 13. Because the App collects no personal data at all, there is no special risk to younger users, but the App is intended for general audiences.

08

Changes to this policy

If this Privacy Policy is updated, the new version will be made available on this page with an updated date. We will not reduce your privacy protections without clear notice.

09

Contact

If you have questions about privacy or this policy, you can reach us at moodyjournalapp@gmail.com.
